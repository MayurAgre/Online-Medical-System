Babu Jagjivan Ram Hospital

Babu Jagjivan Ram Memorial Hospital is a 100 bedded multispeciality District Hospital situated in the E-block, Jahangirpuri, North-West district of Delhi. It is a general secondary level hospital. Its main objective is to provide, promotive & curative health care services. Standards of services may be influenced by workload & availability of resources at that particular time. Yet it is intended that all users receive courteous & prompt attention. The hospital campus is spread over 41780.00 Square meters. The hospital has been established with a view to provide primary, secondary and tertiary level of treatment for people of the rural area of North-West District of Delhi. The hospital has scope for future expansion..

3 Tesla MRI Machine
Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) is a test that uses a magnetic field and pulses of radio wave energy to make pictures of organs and structures inside the body. In many cases, MRI gives more in-depth information about structures in the body than can be seen with an X-ray, ultrasound, or computed tomography (CT) scan.
